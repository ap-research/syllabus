Chapter 2 Course Description

Prerequisite: AP Seminar
AP Research is a one-year course that culminates in a 4000- to 5000-word academic paper and a 15- to 20-minute presentation with oral defense. Students will learn the process of academic research as well as industry-aligned research tools.
