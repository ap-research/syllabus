AP速 Research Syllabus

2 Course Objectives

  1. Develop research proposal that demonstrates ability to define research topic and question with accompanying literature review.
  2. Design proper research procedures that apply sound methodology to address research question.
  3. Analyze and evaluate research findings with appropriate methods.
  4. Apply general research tools such as source management software and version control to organize academic paper.
  5. Gain familiarity with academic and industry standards of reproducible research.
  6. Communicate research effectively with well crafted presentation slides.
